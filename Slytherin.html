Slytherin

Slytherin was founded by Salazar Slytherin. In establishing the house, Salazar instructed the Sorting Hat to pick students who had a few particular characteristics he most valued. Those characteristics included cunning, resourcefulness, leadership, and ambition.
The founder highly valued and favored pure-blood students and the Sorting Hat admitted that it could be a factor when being sorted.
The emblematic animal of the house is a snake and the house's colors are green and silver. The patron ghost of the house is the Bloody Baron.
